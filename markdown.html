2020 LavaCon


LavaCon began in Hawaii as a way to give senior content professionals a way to enhance their own professional development, improve content management initiatives, and stay relevant with everchanging publishing technology. Due to COVID-19, this year's conference was offered virtually for attendees.

Students of WRIT5662 were asked to watch a handful of presentations offered "On Demand" at this year's LavaCon.
